Hardangerfjord Getaway

Mark the date: 23rd of May to 25th of May (Saturday to Pentecost Monday)

We're going to see the apple (hopefully) tree blossom in Hardangerfjord and stay at the Kinsarvik Camping.

We'll stay the whole family in a "Deluxe Bungalow" with sea view. Fruktstien at Lofthus is a 15 minute-drive away.

A motorboat is available to rent. We'll probably do some hiking, maybe biking, and of course, enjoy the beautiful tree blossom.
